Sundials

How they work

They use the sunlight to cast a shadow on the the pole like part called the gnomon The shadow is collected on a base called the dial which has many numbers, and depending on where the shadow is on the base, they can get a rough estimate of what time it is

Origin

The sundial was found being used as early as 1500 BC in Egypt. These early sundials worked differently as they used the length of the shadow, not the location to find the time.
